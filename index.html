Forex

Forex is a global market for trading currencies, where investors buy and sell currency pairs to profit from exchange rate fluctuations. It's the largest financial market, open 24/6, offering ample trading opportunities.

Currencies are quoted in pairs like EUR/USD or GBP/JPY, with the first currency being the base and the second the quote. The exchange rate shows how much of the quote currency is needed to buy one unit of the base currency.

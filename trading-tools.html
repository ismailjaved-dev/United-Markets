Fundamental analysis involves evaluating micro and macro-economic factors that can influence trade performance. By understanding these factors, traders can make informed decisions about their investments based on real-world events and economic trends.

Technical analysis involves using historical price and volume data to identify patterns and trends in the market. Traders use technical analysis tools to predict future price movements and make informed decisions about when to buy or sell assets.

Market sentiment analysis provides insights into the emotions and attitudes of traders towards particular assets. By understanding market sentiment, traders can gauge potential market movements and adjust their strategies accordingly.
